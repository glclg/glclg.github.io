Strategy Powered by Design

Glocal combines design and business strategy to create comprehensive solutions and facility business model renovation. Business strategy powered by design helps business to improve product, service, logistic on a new level.

Detect: from investigation to insight

Through investigation, market analysis, and strategy evaluation, we deeply study a business' target customer, competitors, brand image and major opportunities.

Strategy: from insight to strategy

According to the insight, we utilize finance and capital market resources, and technical skills to build comprehensive strategy.

Design: from strategy to form

We generate design concepts and principles to address challenges and opportunities. All concepts will be tested before we reach a design and development direction.

Strategy and Design Based on Data

Geographic Information System (GIS) is the application of data on space. Based on data analysis, data visualization, and programming, Glocal integrate data into strategy, design, and decision making. Through data, we build a platform for finance, engineering, policy, and design to facilitate renovation for city and real estate.

Glocal: Service and Focus

Glocal services include city and real estate strategy, real estate investment consulting, and design.
Glocal has solid experience in hospitality and tourism property, senior living, central business district (CBD), commercial real estate, education and art properties, and U.S. housing market.

Hospitality and leisure facilities

Glocal's hospitality and leisure facilities consulting includes valuation, market study, and feasibility study, which are based on competitive advantage, cash flow, and long term value.
Glocal's design of Bayhood Golf facilities entered Professional Golfers' Association (PGA) Show Asia. In 2013, Glocal started a feasibility study for Lijiang Snow Mountain Festival, the first large scale outdoor music festival in China.

Medical and Senior Living facilities

Glocal built strong ties with the medical sector. Glocal invested in a medical service chain in China, and carried out a study on the allocation and design standard for the business.
Glocal studies the senior living market, business model, and the cooperation of management company and real estate developer. From 2013, Glocal started the consulting and design for a continuing care retirement community (CCRC) in Harbin, China.
Picture: CCRC in Harbin, China; 2014

Education and Art Properties

Glocal has strong resources in education and art in the United States and China, and delivers unique solutions in education and art properties.
Glocal conducted feasibility for the property of Lijiang Snow Mountain Music Festival. From 2013, Glocal finished the urban design of the 43-hectare campus of Northeast Agricultural University in Harbin, China. We designed the library for the campus.
Picture: 43-hectare campus urban design

United States Housing Market

We help clients to find the right property, make financial plans, and help with each procedure, including site visiting, contract negotiation, house inspection, ownership inspection, transaction, loan, and leasing management. Our one-stop service empowers individual buyer with a professional advisor team.
Glocal helps client to study the environment, security, racial composition, and income level of an area.

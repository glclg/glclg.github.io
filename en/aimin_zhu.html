Aimin Zhu

Mr. Aimin Zhu has more than 20 years' experience in hospitality management. He was deeply involved in the founding and management of the earliest international five-star hotel in China. From 1980s, he was the human resource director of Sheraton Shanghai. He also served as the vice president, human resource director of Mingcheng hotel management company, Oriental Hotel management hotel, etc. He has extensive network in China's hospitality industry.
